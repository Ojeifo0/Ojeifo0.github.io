Soccer vs Football

For years it's been North America vs the world when it comes to the nomenclature of the most played sport in the world. Surely a sport where 11 players pass around a ball with their feet in an attempt to score should be called football, right?

Brief history of the word "Soccer"

It's been known for quite some time that the term "soccer" was a short-form derivative of association football. There have been plenty of people outside of the United States that have refused to admit it, but a primary source confirming it is substantial for the debate.

This won't change much, though. Europeans will continue arguing it should only be called football. U.S. residents will call it soccer so as to not confuse anything with American football.
